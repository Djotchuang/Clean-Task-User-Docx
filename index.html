Getting Started #back to top

How to Add a New User #back to top

You can add a user to the platform through an invitation. This invitation is sent to them through email as a link. When they click on this link, they will be redirected to a registration form on the platform, where they can fill in their details and are automatically added.

The details of sending an invitation is shown below:

  1. Click the invite user button
  2. Fill in the user's email in the box and click the 'invite user' button.

Activate and Deactivate a User #back to top

Deactivating a user will disable them from logging into the platform. The opposite is true for activating a user.
To deactivate a user, click on the red button. Click on the button again (now green) to activate the user.

How to Add a Client #back to top

Clients can be included to a team/project so that they can view the progress of their work. To do so, you can send them an invitation link through email, inviting them to register on the platform by filling in their details.

The procedure is shown below:

  1. Click the invite client button
  2. Fill in the client's email in the box and click the 'invite user' button.

Activate and Deactivate a Client #back to top

Deactivating a client will disable them from logging into the platform, hence they won't be able to view the progress of their projects. Activating a client will enable them to view their project progress.
To deactivate a client, click on the button. Click on the button again to activate the client.

Settings Configuration #back to top

A few settings are available for customization.

Frontend Settings

  • To change the platform name, just type the new name and save the settings.
  • To modify the website primary color, click on the color and select from the available colors. The same can be done for the default projects background color.

Notice Message

You can set a default notice message for users under you team. Check the appropriate box to determine which group of users receive the message.
You can also change the background color of the display, the message title, icon and body.

Manipulating Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) #back to top

FAQs are useful for those using the platform. You can add FAQs to the platform through the FAQ tab. Use the 'add a faq' button to do so. You can also edit or delete a FAQ.

Knowledge Base #back to top

The Knowledge is where users (members of a project) will go to in order to get technical knowledge and awareness on the topics which concern your team and/or project. You can add categories and content to it. You can also edit or delete a category (topic).

Please visit the Knowledge Base section of the user guide to know more.
